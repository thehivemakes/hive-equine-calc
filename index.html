About EquineCalc

EquineCalc is a suite of free calculators built specifically for horse owners, barn managers, and equine professionals. Estimate weight without a scale, plan feed rations, calculate medication doses, figure pasture requirements, and assess body condition.

Every tool runs in your browser with no data leaving your device. Share results via URL — text them to your vet or barn manager.
