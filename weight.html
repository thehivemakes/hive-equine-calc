How to Estimate Horse Weight

Knowing your horse's weight is essential for proper feeding, medication dosing, and health monitoring. Without a livestock scale, the weight tape formula provides a reliable estimate.

Measure heart girth (circumference around the barrel just behind the front legs) and body length (from the point of the shoulder to the point of the buttock). The formula Heart girth² × Body length ÷ 330 gives weight in pounds.
