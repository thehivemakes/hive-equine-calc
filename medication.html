Common Equine Medication Doses

Accurate medication dosing is critical for horse health. Most equine drugs are dosed by body weight. Under-dosing reduces effectiveness; over-dosing can cause toxicity.

This calculator provides standard dosing guidelines for common equine medications. Always verify doses with your veterinarian, especially for prescription drugs and any horse with health conditions.
